## The whole plan in one sentence

> **Learn first, practice small, grow your earning skill, invest gradually, and let surplus naturally pay down debt — not the other way around.**

## The 5 learning stages (preview)

1. **Stage 0 — Learn the language** (Months 1–2): grasp 10 core concepts, no money moves yet
2. **Stage 1 — Practice tracking** (Month 2–3): log your real expenses + income, see your true picture
3. **Stage 2 — Build the earning side** (Month 3–9): niche, raise rates, productize your skill
4. **Stage 3 — Small investing experiments** (Month 6–18): DPS 500/mo, sanchaypatra, 1k/mo in halal stocks
5. **Stage 4 — Deeper wealth knowledge** (Year 2+): financial statements, valuation, halal finance frameworks
6. **Stage 5 — Compounding wealth + clean debt** (Year 3+): by now surplus is real, debt clears naturally

Read `03-the-learning-roadmap.md` for the full version.

## What this folder is NOT

- It's not a debt-payoff war plan. Debt is one chapter, not the book.
- It's not a get-rich-quick scheme.
- It's not an investing course. You'll learn investing — but only when you've built the foundation.
- It's not pressure. It's a long, patient, knowledge-led path.

## How to use this folder

1. **Today (20 min):** read `01-where-you-are.md`, then `03-the-learning-roadmap.md`
2. **This week:** start `06-week-1-learning.md` — small concrete steps
3. **Daily (15 min):** open `tracker.html` in a browser, log expenses, study one concept
4. **Weekly (Sunday, 30 min):** review the week, plan study for the next
5. **Monthly:** read the next file in the sequence, internalize one big idea

The pace is gentle. The path is long. The compound effect is real.

> **"You don't get rich by paying off debt. You get rich by building knowledge and skill that earns more than it spends. Debt is a symptom, not the disease."**
