# 02 — How `doc.md` Relates to This Roadmap

The original `doc.md` is what kicked this whole conversation off. It says, basically:

> Financial skills build wealth more reliably than tech skills alone. Tech earns income, finance turns income into wealth. Here are three tiers of finance to learn (foundation → wealth-building → advanced). Avoid trading, MLMs, and gurus. Use your dev skill + Islamic finance interest as a unique edge. Read these books in this order over 3 months.

It's a **learning curriculum**. That's exactly what you asked for. So the relationship is simple:

## `doc.md` is the philosophy. This folder is the operationalization.

`doc.md` says *"learn finance, here's the broad map."*
This folder says *"here is exactly what to do this week, this month, this year — to actually learn it."*

If `doc.md` is the map of a country, the files in this folder are the daily itinerary. Same destination, much more usable.

## Where this roadmap differs from `doc.md`

A few small but important adjustments:

**Pace.** `doc.md` suggests a 3-month learning compress. This folder spreads it across 18–24 months. Faster is fine for someone with lots of free time and no other obligations; for you, with freelance work + family + a real life, sustainable pace beats sprint.

**Concept depth before action.** `doc.md` recommends opening an investment account in Month 3. This roadmap holds investing until Stage 3 (Month 6+) — long enough for you to genuinely understand what you're investing in, why, and at what risk.

**Adds tracking practice.** `doc.md` doesn't really cover the *practice* of seeing your own numbers — expense tracking, net worth logging, etc. This folder adds those because reading without practice doesn't stick. The `tracker.html` is your practice tool.

**Honest about debt as context.** `doc.md` doesn't mention debt much. This folder adds `10-debt-awareness.md` as a chapter — not the whole book — because you do have debt, and ignoring it forever isn't sustainable. But it's positioned correctly: a thing to know about and slowly address as your skill and surplus grow, not the structural priority.

**Same Islamic finance edge.** The unique opportunity `doc.md` identifies — your potential in halal fintech / Islamic finance products — stays. It's just spread across Stage 2 (income work) and Stage 5 (wealth knowledge), where it actually fits naturally.

## What to do with `doc.md` itself

Keep it. Re-read it once you finish Stage 1 of this roadmap. By then you'll have:
- Real tracking data
- A grasp of foundational concepts
- Some early income experiments

…and `doc.md` will read very differently. The advice that felt abstract when you first saw it will feel obvious and actionable.

## One-line summary

> **`doc.md` told you what to learn. This folder shows you how to learn it without giving up your life to do it.**
