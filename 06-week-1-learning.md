# 06 — First Week: Concrete Learning Actions

Not "fix everything." Not "face the damage." Just *start*. Small steps, real practice, foundational learning. By Day 7 you'll already know more than 80% of adults around you.

---

## Day 1 (today)
- [ ] Open `tracker.html` in a browser. Bookmark it. (Or save a shortcut to your phone home screen.)
- [ ] Read `01-where-you-are.md` honestly. No defensiveness, no panic.
- [ ] Read `03-the-learning-roadmap.md` once through.
- [ ] Watch one Adib's Finance or 10 Minute School finance video (≤30 min)
- [ ] Tell your spouse: *"I'm starting a 12–24 month learning journey on money. I'll explain it tonight."*

**Goal:** orient yourself. No money moves. No tracking yet. Just take the map in.

---

## Day 2
- [ ] Open `09-concepts.md`. Read the entry on **Cash flow** and **Net worth**. Slow.
- [ ] In your own words, write a one-paragraph explanation of each. (Notes tab in tracker, or paper notebook — anywhere.)
- [ ] Watch one short video on each topic (search: "cash flow Bangla", "net worth meaning Bangla")
- [ ] Install Wallet or Money Lover on your phone — to be ready for Stage 1

**Goal:** 2 concepts understood. That's plenty for one day.

---

## Day 3
- [ ] Read `09-concepts.md` entries on **Compound interest** and **Inflation**
- [ ] Write your own explanation of each
- [ ] Look up Bangladesh's current inflation rate (BBS website or quick search). Note it in your notes — this is your real inflation reality.
- [ ] Tick off the 4 concepts you've learned this week in the tracker's **Concepts** tab

**Goal:** by end of Day 3, 4 of the 10 foundational concepts are inside your head.

---

## Day 4
- [ ] Read `09-concepts.md` entries on **Real return** and **Time value of money**
- [ ] In Notes tab: write a 3-sentence story showing what these mean. Example: *"If I keep 100 taka under my mattress, in 5 years it can buy what 65 taka buys today — because inflation eats the rest. That's why money sitting still loses real value."*
- [ ] Watch one video on each (Khan Academy Bangla has good ones)

**Goal:** the two trickiest concepts under your belt.

---

## Day 5
- [ ] Read `09-concepts.md` entries on **Asset vs Liability** and **Income vs Wealth**
- [ ] List 5 things you own and label them: asset (puts money in pocket) vs liability (takes money out). Most people are surprised how little they own that's actually an asset.
- [ ] In Notes tab: write what "wealth" means to you in your own words (not Instagram's version)

**Goal:** start seeing your own life through the asset/liability lens.

---

## Day 6
- [ ] Read `09-concepts.md` entries on **Risk vs Return** and **Halal vs Haram Finance**
- [ ] Note down 3 examples of "guaranteed high return" schemes you've seen on Facebook/YouTube — recognize them as the lies they are
- [ ] Look up one halal Bangladeshi bank's website (IBBL, Al-Arafah). Just browse. Get familiar with terms like Mudaraba, Sukuk, Wadiah. No accounts yet.

**Goal:** 8 of 10 concepts in. Risk literacy + halal awareness on top.

---

## Day 7 — Family Sunday (1 hour together)
- [ ] Show your spouse the tracker dashboard
- [ ] Explain what you've been learning this week — teach the concepts to them. Teaching is the deepest form of learning.
- [ ] Together: open the tracker's **Expenses tab**. Make a pact: starting tomorrow, you'll both log every taka spent for 30 days. (Stage 1 starts.)
- [ ] Schedule a **weekly Sunday 30-min money chat** going forward. Calendar reminder.
- [ ] Tick the last 2 concepts (covered or to-come) so the Concepts tab shows your Week 1 progress

**Goal:** spouse aligned. Tracking habit ready to begin Day 8. 10 foundational concepts mostly internalized.

---

## End of Week 1 — what you should have

- ✅ 10 finance concepts understood at a basic level (and tracked in Concepts tab)
- ✅ Tracker bookmarked and open daily
- ✅ Spouse aligned
- ✅ Weekly Sunday check-in scheduled
- ✅ Wallet / Money Lover app installed and ready
- ✅ Notes tab has 5+ entries in your own words
- ✅ Confidence that you can actually do this gentle path

**Crucially: you did NOT do any of these in Week 1:**
- ❌ Start attacking your debt
- ❌ Open an investment account
- ❌ Make any "big" money decision
- ❌ Panic about the past

You spent Week 1 *learning*. That's the whole point. The rest of the roadmap stacks on top of this foundation.

Stage 1 begins — practice tracking. See `03-the-learning-roadmap.md` for what that looks like.
